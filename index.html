WELCOME TO INDRYANI SHIKSHAN SANSTHA'S ARTS SCIENCE & COMMERECE COLLEGE

Vision

The college envisions to be an academic community for holistic education of future leaders who will demonstrate innovation in their professional competencies, integration in their personal lives and inclusion in their social contribution, and having continuous Research & Development centers in all departments, also Centre for excellence in skill training, placement and career development.

Mission

To provide educational opportunities to all aspiring youth to excel in life by nurturing academic superiority,fostering values, creating civic responsibility, inculcating environmental concern and building global competencies in a dynamic environment.

About Indrayani shikshan Sanstha

Indrayani Shikshan Sanstha established in the year 1999 to contribute in the field of higher education in India. From the very beginning, the trust tried all its best to emphasis on the education of the down-trodden, the poor and the ignorant who really form the major portion of the society. The trustees envisioned to empower the masses by devoting their mind and heart to the cause of education. They believed that education alone could correct the social ills such as caste-hierarchy, poverty, superstitions and social and economic inequality. The trust focuses on the growth of educational institution in Mumbai and its suburbs to foster socio-economic growth of the people.

Indrayani Shikshan Sanstha’s Arts, Science and Commerce College, Dwarli, Kalyan (E), Dist-Thane, Maharashtra is affiliated to University of Mumbai. It is run by Indrayani Shikshan Sanstha, Kalyan, Dist-Thane, founded by the visionary Hon Secretary Prof. (Dr.) Vilas B. Gaikar, and Dr. Leena Vilas Gaikar, Hon. Chairman of the trust. The college relishes the dream of socio-economic upliftment of the rural and urban masses who are socially and economically underprivileged and with an aim of their overall development by the means of education. The college was established with the noble vision and students will benefit from the quality education provided by the college and enhance in academic, social, political, cultural and sports life etc. The college will offer traditional programmes like B.A., B.Com. and B.Sc. at UG level as per regulations by the government of Maharashtra and University of Mumbai from time to time. The college will implement the National Education Policy 2020 as per guidelines. The academic programmes and career-oriented courses offered by the college will prove beneficial in mounting placement of the students in different fields. Extension activities also to be carried out under NSS, NCC, Health Centre, Arts Forum, and Women Development Cell are distinctive features of the college and its commitment towards societal responsibilities. The college will conduct various outreach activities to help of its support services to instil life skills and core values among the students. It will also take care of the students’ engagement in research activities through research Conventions and other research activities like paper/poster presentations, participation in conferences/seminars/workshops, training program etc. The college will also focus on its infrastructure and facilities to cope with the changing scenario of higher education and modern demands in the context of globalization. The teaching-learning and evaluation process of the college to be supported with conducive educational atmosphere and ideal infrastructural facilities. The college has adequate number of classroom and well-equipped Administrative Office, Science Laboratories, Departmental Libraries, Placement Cell, Competitive Examination Centre, Examination Sections, Smart Classroom, Common room for Boys’ and Girls’, Canteen, Parking facility, ICT equipment etc. to facilitate the curricular, co-curricular and extra-curricular activities. Student Support and their progression to higher education to be looked after by the college sincerely. It will offer welfare schemes for both the staff and students. Earn and Learn Scheme, Management Scholarships, Book Bank Scheme, transportation facility, academic and Career Counselling, Guidance to avail government scholarships, Job Fair, Payment of Admission Fees in instalments, Special Incentives to the NCC cadets, NSS Volunteers and sport persons, etc. are some of the remarkable mechanisms/schemes to support the students. The college will collaborate for MoU, agreements for academic excellence with the Government and NGOs through which training programs, national and international conferences/ seminars, internships and job prospects to be achieved by the learners.
